About Smart Parking

Smart Parking is a modern solution designed to help drivers locate and manage available parking slots in real-time. Whether you're a daily commuter or a parking manager, our system makes it easier to find and manage parking efficiently.

Built using Django and our system bridges the gap between technology and convenience. Our goal is to reduce traffic, save time, and simplify urban transportation.

This platform is part of an innovative hackathon project developed by passionate developers aiming to solve real-world problems.
